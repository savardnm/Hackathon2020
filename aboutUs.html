What is This?

Imagine you're a chemical engineering major, trying to figure out how to
make a distillation column at the end of B Term your sophomore year but
your group has nowhere to go because Foisie is full (and faculty tried
steal it from you anyways so whatever) and the library is full and
you have one hour until your next class but you spent half of it
trying to find somewhere with an outlet to sit down because your laptop
battery has died completely and life is just really hard right now
and you spent an hour crying on the phone to your mom last night
but you're still out here doing your best but it's not your best
because your computer is dead and every study space is full.
Now imagine hecking the system and studying in empty classrooms
between class where there are outlets and quiet space and not
your private hell.

That's what us, a team of quirky and
loveable family friendly WPI students come in!
We made this app during 2020 Hackathon for you!
